LostSierra Apps ("we," "our," or "us") operates the ChoreQuest mobile application and web application (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

By using ChoreQuest, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
- **Name**: Display name for family members
- **Email Address**: Required for parent accounts using Google Sign-In
- **User Role**: Whether you are a parent or child user
- **Birthdate**: Optional information for child accounts (used for age-appropriate features)
- **Avatar/Profile Picture**: Optional profile image

### 1.2 Authentication Information
- **Google Account Information**: When parents sign in with Google, we receive basic profile information (name, email) through Google OAuth 2.0
- **Authentication Tokens**: Encrypted tokens used for QR code authentication
- **Device Information**: Device ID, device name, and last active timestamp for each device logged into your account

### 1.3 Chore and Task Data
- **Chore Titles and Descriptions**: Tasks created by parents
- **Chore Assignments**: Which family members are assigned to each chore
- **Completion Status**: Whether chores are pending, in progress, completed, or verified
- **Due Dates**: Scheduled completion dates for chores
- **Subtasks**: Breakdown of chore steps
- **Recurring Schedules**: Information about repeating chores
- **Photos**: Images uploaded as proof of chore completion (stored securely)

### 1.4 Rewards and Points Data
- **Reward Information**: Titles, descriptions, and point costs of rewards
- **Point Transactions**: History of points earned and spent
- **Redemption Status**: Whether reward redemptions are pending, approved, or denied

### 1.5 Activity and Usage Data
- **Activity Logs**: Comprehensive audit trail of all actions taken in the app, including:
  - Chore creation, editing, completion, and verification
  - Point transactions (earnings, spending, adjustments)
  - Reward creation and redemption
  - User account changes
  - Device login and logout events
- **User Statistics**: Total chores completed, current streak days
- **App Settings**: Theme preferences, notification settings, celebration styles, sound effect preferences

### 1.6 Technical Information
- **Device Information**: Device type (Android, Web), device identifiers
- **Network Information**: Connection status for syncing data
- **Usage Patterns**: How you interact with the app (for app functionality only)

## 3. How We Store Your Information

### 3.1 Data Storage Location
**All data collected by ChoreQuest is stored exclusively in the primary parent's Google Drive account.** LostSierra Apps does not retain, store, or maintain any copies of your data on our servers or systems.

Your data is stored securely in **Google Drive** through our Google Apps Script backend service, which acts only as a conduit to read and write data to your Google Drive. This includes:
- Family information and settings
- User accounts and profiles
- Chores and assignments
- Rewards and transactions
- Activity logs
- Uploaded photos

All data is stored in JSON format within a private folder in the primary parent's Google Drive account. The data remains under your control and ownership at all times.

### 3.2 Session Management
The app stores encrypted authentication tokens locally on your device using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences. These tokens are used solely for authentication purposes and do not contain your personal data.

### 3.3 Data Security
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:
- **Encryption**: Authentication tokens and sensitive data are encrypted
- **Secure Authentication**: Uses industry-standard OAuth 2.0 and encrypted QR codes
- **Access Controls**: Only authenticated family members can access their family's data
- **Secure Storage**: Data stored using Google Drive's security infrastructure
